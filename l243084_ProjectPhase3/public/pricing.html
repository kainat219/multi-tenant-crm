Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers

Can I upgrade my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Is my data safe and isolated?

Absolutely. Each tenant's data is completely isolated. No other tenant can access your data.

How does the Free Trial work?

You get full access to basic features for 14 days with up to 2 users and 20 contacts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers for all subscription plans.
